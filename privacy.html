Triglavis Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 31, 2025

Overview

Triglavis LLC is a software company that develops and operates multiple applications and services. We are committed to protecting your privacy - in fact, most of our products don't collect any personal data at all. This privacy policy applies to all products and services offered by Triglavis LLC.

Information We Collect

Most of our products do not collect any personal information. However, we may collect information in these limited circumstances:

  • Contact information when you reach out for support via phone or email
  • Payment processing handled by third-party providers (e.g., LemonSqueezy) - we do not store payment details
  • Information you voluntarily provide when contacting us for business inquiries

Individual products may have their own privacy practices, which will be disclosed separately if they differ from this policy.

How We Use Your Information

When we do collect information, we use it only to:

  • Respond to your support requests and inquiries
  • Process transactions through our payment providers
  • Send communications you've explicitly requested
  • Comply with legal obligations

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties except:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To trusted service providers who assist in operating our platform
  • When required by law or to protect our rights
  • In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

Communication Preferences

By providing your contact information, you consent to receive:

  • Account verification and security notifications
  • Service updates and important announcements
  • Customer support communications
  • SMS notifications for account security (when phone number provided)
  • Product-specific updates for services you use

You may opt out of non-essential communications at any time by replying STOP to SMS messages or contacting support.

Data Security

Since we collect minimal information, your privacy is protected by design. When we do handle information:

  • Support communications are kept confidential
  • Payment processing is handled by certified third-party providers
  • We do not store sensitive payment information
  • Access to any collected information is strictly limited

Data Retention

We retain minimal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, comply with legal obligations, or resolve disputes.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and update your account information
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Opt out of certain communications
  • Request a copy of your data

Third-Party Services

Our products and services may integrate with third-party services. This privacy policy does not cover the practices of third parties. We encourage you to read their privacy policies.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Contact Us
